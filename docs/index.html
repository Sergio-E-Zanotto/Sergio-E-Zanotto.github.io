I Started my PhD in October 2021 as a PhD candidate in Linguistics and an Independent Doctoral Fellow at the Cluster of Excellence "The Politics of Inequality", University of Konstanz.

My work implies the creation and the analysis of a corpus of Italian political discourses. I would like to shed lights on which linguistic features are more relevant in attracting political preferences among different strata of the population.
