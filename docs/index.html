Sergio Eugenio Zanotto

PhD Candidate in Linguistics

Who I am

I started my PhD in October 2021 as a PhD candidate in Linguistics and an Independent Doctoral Fellow at the Cluster of Excellence "The Politics of Inequality," University of Konstanz.

My Interests

My work involves the creation and analysis of a corpus of Italian political discourses. I aim to understand which linguistic features are more relevant in attracting political preferences among different strata of the population.
